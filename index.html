About us

Noval Waste Management is an IIM (Indian Institute of Management) alumni enterprise focussed on comprehensive waste management and environmental services. Noval Waste Management is committed to provide innovative and engineered solutions for communities, organisations, industries, households and municipalities to manage waste.

We are a specialised company who implements on-site solid waste disposal methods. We evaluate waste streams through a comprehensive and extensive waste audit. Our experts layout a detailed solution to handle different waste streams

At Noval, we are committed to develop engineered solutions that can help communities and organizations achieve their green goals, including zero waste. We call it Responsible Engineering.

GREEN BURNER

NOVAL Green burner is an innovation in waste management. Green burner from NOVAL converts waste to ash without using electricity or fossil fuels. It uses the calorific value present in waste to produce heat energy which converts waste to ash in a matter of hours. The high temperature involved in the combustion process converts the waste to ash, flue gas and heat. This process reduces solid mass of waste by85% and volume by 95%.

Waste burners are single chambered units. Waste is raised off the bottom of the box by placing it on grates inside the unit. Ash formed after burning falls through the grate making removal easier once a sufficient amount has accumulated. Combustion air is provided by draft ports at the bottom of the box allowing for better mixing with the burning waste. The waste is exposed to natural drafts through the metal grating on all surfaces including the bottom. This enables air to better mix with burning waste and promotes more efficient combustion throughout the burning period. The high alumina and silica refractory lining provides heat retention for longer duration.

NOVAL waste burners are equipped with specially made curved refractories for enhanced compression resistance during expansion due to heat and thus improving the refractory lining life drastically.

ORGANIC WASTE CONVERTER

Organic Waste Converter (OWC) is all about converting Organic Waste to Organic Fertilizer called Compost, in the fastest possible time. What would take 2-3 months to get composted by the natural process, NOVAL OWC should be able to compost it in 10-15 days!

Feed the shredded organic waste to the OWC. Add Bioculum and absorbent as required to the waste fed in OWC. Bioculum is a mixture of micro organism cultures that accelerate the aerobic composting of bio degradable organic waste. It also treats the waste and makes it free from pathogens, foul smells and weed seeds. It facilitates the rapid conversion of organic waste into a bio stabilized compost. It is free from any toxic or hazardous components. It preserves the vital nutrients and organic matter in the waste.

OWC then processes the waste in 15 minutes. The processed waste is then taken out from OWC and placed into a curing system for further treatment. In 10-15 days the waste is converted into compost and can be sold in the market as organic fertilizer.
